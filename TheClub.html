How our points work

Our members enjoy the most flexible and valuable travel points program around.
   

Great earning potential

Receive points called Member Rewards every time you book your stay with us. All our hotels are eligible so the more you stay, the better the rewards.

1 Member Reward = US$1

Earn up to 7% of the total amount of your booking. Your points can be used as soon as you’ve completed your stay.

  

Spend when it suits you

With no waiting and blackout periods, you can spend your Member Rewards whenever you like, on any hotel room in the world.

Even more with our app

Enjoy more convenience, receive mobile-only offers wherever you are inthe world and earn more Member Rewards whenever you book via the app.
